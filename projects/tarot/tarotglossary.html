Thaís' Tarot Corner
Major Arcana
The first 21 cards of the tarot, usually indicate overarching themes, rather than specific events.
Minor Arcana
Cards from 1-10, plus court cards, of each suit, usually refer to more short-term events, rather than trends or major occurrences.
Court Cards

The page, knight, queen and king of each suit. They usually symbolize a person related to the question, either an external influence or the querent themselves.
To learn more about Court Cards, check out this episode of Biddy Tarot's podcast.
